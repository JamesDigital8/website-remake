About Us

We believe passionately in helping real estate agencies and other business owners around New Zealand maximise every sales or service opportunity they get. Providing advice, analytics, ongoing support, increased awareness either locally, nationwide, even globally. FIMA prides itself on being a where and when required resource for your organisation.
